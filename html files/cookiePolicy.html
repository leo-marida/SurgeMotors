Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store on your device to enhance your browsing experience. They help us remember your preferences and improve our website’s performance.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure website functionality and security.
  • Remember user preferences (language, currency, etc.).
  • Analyze website traffic and improve user experience.
  • Provide personalized content and targeted advertisements.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Our website uses the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality.
  • Performance Cookies: Help analyze traffic and improve our website.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used for personalized ads and promotions.
  • Functional Cookies: Store user preferences and settings.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may impact your user experience.

5. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page.
