Data Protection Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Data Protection Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Full Name, Email, Phone Number
  • Billing & Shipping Address
  • Payment Details (Encrypted)
  • Car Purchase & Service History
  • IP Address, Browser Type, and Device Information

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Process vehicle purchases and services
  • Provide customer support
  • Send marketing offers (if consented)
  • Improve website functionality
  • Ensure security and fraud prevention

4. Data Security & Protection

We implement strict security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption of payment and personal data
  • Two-Factor Authentication for login security
  • Secure servers and firewalls
  • Regular security audits and compliance checks

5. Your Rights

Under data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request deletion or correction of data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • File a complaint if you believe your data is misused

6. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share information with:

  • Payment processors for transactions
  • Government authorities if legally required
  • Partners providing financing or insurance services

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, contact us at support@cardealership.com
